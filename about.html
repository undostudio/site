undo.studio

undo.studio makes software that you’ll enjoy using.

We’ve found that products* are enjoyable when they are

  1. simple—not a higher level of abstraction, but rather no abstraction. When tools are simple at the core, they are easy to understand, reliable. They work as if they'll continue to work for a hundred years.
  2. fast—software should not feel slower than it was forty years ago.
  3. elegant—crafted, created to a degree of unreasonable quality.
  4. indie—when creators are supported directly by the people using their products, there’s more at stake for the creator, more need for that unreasonable quality.
  5. focused—small, sharp, and solves a problem really well. The product is informed by the problem, not the other way around.

* The terms software, product, and tool are used interchangeably, as most software is a means to an end.
