undo.studio

A product and research studio, crafting software and typefaces, and exploring ideas in language and human-computer interaction.

Not a team, just one person trying to answer the question, can I make a business building tools that people enjoy using?

There are a few things that I feel go into building well-crafted products: opinionated, favor simplicity through less abstraction, not more, have as few dependencies as possible, and act as an ambient medium through which people accomplish their goal. This is not the only way to build, but it’s one way.
