undo.studio

A product and research studio, crafting software and typefaces, and exploring ideas in language and human-computer interaction.

Not a team, just one person trying to make software a bit better.

I build products that are opinionated, that favor simplicity through less abstraction, not more, and that use as few dependencies as possible. This is not the only way to build, but it has its benefits.

I also have an interest in making ambient tools that act as a transparent medium through which the user accomplishes their goals.
